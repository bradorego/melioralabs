Why Meliora

Where it came from, what it means, and why we're doing it.

  • Fall 2006

    An Unlikely Combination

    MelioraLabs founder Brad Orego starts his studies at the University of Rochester, intending to double-major in Computer Science and Psychology. Brad had no idea what user-centered design was until he took a topical class called Human Computer Interaction in Fall 2008.

  • Early 2012

    The Acorn Falls

    After completing his Take 5 and graduating, Brad moved to Madison, WI, to start work at Healthcare IT giant Epic Systems. After realizing his passion for user-centered design and creating change was being underutilized, he left Epic and immediately got involved in Madison's burgeoning startup scene, leading a team at Startup Weekend: Madison 2012 and mentoring at 3-Day Startup: Madison 2012.

  • Early 2013

    Taking the Leap

    Having spent the insuing year creating incredible interactive experiences with ZebraDog and building his network, Brad leaves the comfort, security, and salary to stake out on his own. He immediately begins working with early-stage companies in the Madison area.

  • Summer 2016

    Meliora is Born

    To say MelioraLabs was created is somewhat of a misnomer. Meliora has always existed in the hearts of designers everywhere. Our goal is to help companies create the best product possible by leveraging concepts from Psychology and User-Centered Design. Meliora isn't just the name, it's what we do. We make things better.

  • Our Mission

    MelioraLabs was founded with two main goals: 1) to provide exemplary service to our clients, and 2) to provide structured learning opportunities for junior designers. By working with ML, not only do you make your products better but you make the design community better.
